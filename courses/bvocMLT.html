B.Voc in Medical Lab Technology

OVERVIEW

For the last few years, the medical industry changed drastically and required more skilled professionals to run smoothly. To support the medical industry, B.Voc in Medical Laboratory Technology (B.Voc. MLT) course is introduced which specializes in an individual performing various clinical tests on patients' clinical specimens. Students who complete this program will have the knowledge and abilities necessary to operate in a clinical laboratory, where they will conduct various tests to identify and cure disorders.

Candidates can pursue specialties like human anatomy and physiology, and courses in virology, histopathology, and computer application. This degree can open up a variety of professional prospects and help you launch a successful career in allied healthcare.

CAREER SCOPE
  • Medical Laboratory Technician :As a medical laboratory technician, graduates can work in hospitals, clinics, and laboratories, where they will be responsible for performing tests and analyzing specimens.
  • Research Assistant : Graduates can also work as research assistants in research institutions, where they will be responsible for conducting experiments, analyzing data, and contributing to research projects.
  • Academia : Graduates can also pursue further studies and work as educators or researchers in academia.
  • Quality Control Officer : Graduates can also work as quality control officers, where they will be responsible for ensuring that laboratories meet regulatory and quality standards.
  • Laboratory Manager : Graduates can also work as laboratory managers, where they will be responsible for managing the operations of a laboratory, including supervising staff, ensuring the laboratory meets regulatory standards, and managing equipment and supplies.
